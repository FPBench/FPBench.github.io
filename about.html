About

FPBench is committed to advancing the field of floating-point research by providing a unified, standardized platform for benchmarks, tools, and practices. Central to our mission is advancing reproducible research in floating-point precision analysis, accuracy, and verification by developing shared infrastructure. Beyond this, FPBench passionately fosters a collaborative community, emphasizing open dialogue, shared resources, and collective growth. FPBench strives to facilitate relationships across the community by creating spaces for researchers to connect, collaborate, and contribute, thus co-creating a vibrant ecosystem driven by mutual respect and shared purpose.
