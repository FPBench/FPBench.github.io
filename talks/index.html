Speakers

FPTalks 2020 will feature industry participants from Google, Microsoft, and Stillwater Supercomputing, and academic researchers from Rutgers, UW, ETH Zurich Utah, and MPI-SWS. If you would like to speak, please get in touch.

Schedule

FPTalks 2020 will take place on 24 June 2020, with talks distributed throughout the day to accommodate time zones and remote participation. Talks will be 10 minutes long and cover recent and ongoing research and results in analysis and design of numerical code and number representations.

Venue

Due to the global pandemic, FPTalks 2020 will be held online. All talks will be recorded.
